About JCC

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) is a renowned conference center located in Jakarta, Indonesia. With a rich legacy spanning several decades, JCC has established itself as one of the earliest and most prestigious convention centers in Jakarta. Since its opening in 1974, JCC has played host to numerous significant national and international conferences, exhibitions, fairs, indoor sports events, and musical concerts.

At JCC, we take pride in our history of hosting landmark events that have shaped the course of history. Notable conferences held at our center include the 11th Non-Aligned Movement Conference and the Asian-African Conference, which have brought together global leaders and thinkers to discuss and address pressing issues of the time.

Throughout the years, JCC has maintained its commitment to excellence, providing a venue that exudes elegance, sophistication, and versatility. Our state-of-the-art facilities, combined with our dedicated team of professionals, ensure that every event held at JCC is executed flawlessly, exceeding the expectations of our esteemed clients and guests.

As we look to the future, our vision is to become an internationally recognized conference center of the highest caliber in Indonesia. With our strategic location, world-class infrastructure, and commitment to delivering exceptional service, we strive to attract a diverse range of national and international events, cementing JCC's position as a premier destination for conferences, exhibitions, and other prestigious gatherings.

Certifications

Jockey's Conference Center (JCC) has been recognized with prestigious awards that highlight our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement in key areas. Some of the notable awards we have received include:

ISO 22000:2018

We have been honored with the ISO 22000 : 2018 certification, which recognizes our dedication to food safety management systems. This award reflects our stringent processes and practices to ensure the highest standards of food safety for our guests and clients.

OHSAS 18001

JCC has been acknowledged with the OHSAS 18001 certification, which validates our commitment to occupational health and safety management. This award signifies our proactive approach to providing a safe and healthy environment for our employees, clients, and visitors.
